Lightning & Master World Championships 2015
Buffalo Canoe Club, Ontario, Canada

Welcome

Welcome to the 2015 Lightning Class International Masters and World Championship! The Buffalo Canoe Club (BCC), its officers, directors, staff, and members look forward to hosting the 2015 Lightning Class International Masters and World Championship July 15-25, 2015. We last hosted the 1973 Lightning World Championship and truly look forward to hosting again. Of course, since that time, the BCC hosted 1977, 1982, 1987, 1992, 1998 and 2004 Lightning North American Championships; 1980, 1990, and 2014 Youth World Championships, as well as numerous Canadian Open and Lake Erie Districts championships. The organizing committee and Lightning Fleet 12 are working hard to make this a first class event. We can’t wait to welcome you at our club.

Camp for Kids

Day Camp is available for children of Worlds Lighting Sailors.

Ages 4-11: The BCC Day Camp offers a variety of experiences: Sailing, Tennis, Swimming, Tubing, Crafts, Games, etc. all on the campus of our beautiful beachfront club. This will be offered Monday July 20 thru Saturday July 25th from 8:30 am until racing is complete each day and sailors return to shore. Sign up for the entire week or individual days. We estimate the cost will be $65/day or $300 for the entire week (6 days). Please pack a lunch.

Ages 10-15: We also offer a sailing program Mon-Fri from 9am to 4pm. We aim to provide a safe, caring environment to develop and promote your child’s physical, creative, intellectual and social skills. We have fantastic staff who are caring, well trained and who are great role models. More information is available on the Buffalo Canoe Club website, or please contact Debbie Probst.
