Amentities

The Buffalo Canoe Club and Crystal Beach have many amenities for sailors and their families.

Camp for Kids

Day Camp is available for children of Worlds Lightning Sailors.

Ages 4-11
The BCC Day Camp offers a variety of experiences: Sailing, Tennis, Swimming, Tubing, Crafts, Games, etc. all on the campus of our beautiful beachfront club. This will be offered Monday July 20 thru Saturday July 25th from 8:30 am until racing is complete each day and sailors return to shore. Sign up for the entire week or individual days. To register your child, please contact Amanda: junioractivities@buffalocc.on.ca and specify "Lightning Worlds Day Camp". $65/day or $300 for the entire week (6 days). Please pack a lunch.

House & Grounds

Dining & Pirate's Den

The BCC is well equipped for dining with a snack bar, formal dining room, and the historic Pirates Den bar. For more information, see the Dining Facilities website.

Fields & Courts

The Buffalo Canoe Club has fields for lacrosse, baseball, and soccer. In addition, there are four tennis courts that are available to sailors and their families. Tennis professionals are on deck for individual or group lessons for the whole family.

Facilities/Amenities

The BCC has extensive shower, bath, and changing room facilities. For more detailed information, pleas visit the BCC website.

Sight-Seeing & Day Trips

There are many great sight-seeing destinations close to the BCC. See the BCC website for more information and itinerary suggestions. Suggested day trips: Niagara Falls (25 minutes), Niagara Gorge (35 minutes), Buffalo (20 minutes), Niagara-on-the Lake & Niagara Wine Region (35 minutes), Toronto (1 hour 30 minutes) Port Colborne & Welland Canal (20 minutes) Fort Erie (15 minutes), Fort George (45 minutes). For more ideas and suggestions, please visit Niagara Region Tourism.
