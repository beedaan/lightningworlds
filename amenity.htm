Amentities

The Buffalo Canoe Club and Crystal Beach have many amenities for sailors and their families.

Camp for Kids

Day Camp is available for children of Worlds Lighting Sailors.

Ages 4-11
The BCC Day Camp offers a variety of experiences: Sailing, Tennis, Swimming, Tubing, Crafts, Games, etc. all on the campus of our beautiful beachfront club. This will be offered Monday July 20 thru Saturday July 25th from 8:30 am until racing is complete each day and sailors return to shore. Sign up for the entire week or individual days. We estimate the cost will be $65/day or $300 for the entire week (6 days). Please pack a lunch.
