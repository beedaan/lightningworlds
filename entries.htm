Qualification

Lightning Masters

The 2015 International Masters Championship regatta is open to all skippers who have reached their 55th birthday by December 31, 2015. Minimum combined age of skipper and crew shall be 130 years as of December 31, 2015. Master skipper must be Life, Active, or Associate members. Crew must be Life, Active, Associate or Crew ILCA members.

Lightning Worlds

Qualification for this regatta will be according to the Document Governing All Sanctioned Lightning Class Championships.
