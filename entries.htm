Entries / Registration

Both world championships require qualification. View the eligibility requirements below.

Qualification

Lightning Masters

The 2015 International Masters Championship regatta is open to all skippers who have reached their 55th birthday by December 31, 2015. Minimum combined age of skipper and crew shall be 130 years as of December 31, 2015. Master skipper must be Life, Active, or Associate members. Crew must be Life, Active, Associate or Crew ILCA members.

Lightning Worlds

Qualification for this regatta will be according to the Document Governing All Sanctioned Lightning Class Championships.
Country Slot Allocation - deadline to confirm country slots is 12/31/14.
US Qualifier Information/ slot allocation - initial invitation sent 10/1/2014.
For other Country Qualification Information please contact your Country VP or the Class Office.

Confirmation

Area VP's must confirm with VP World Championship Rob Ruhlman or ILCA Secretary Laura Jeffers the number of spots (and if possible skipper names) by December 31, 2014. After that time unconfirmed slots will be re-allocated to other areas that have requested additional slots.
